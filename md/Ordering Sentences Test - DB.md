**1\. Arrange the sentences in the correct order:**

A. After several weeks, the villagers began to miss the wise old man.  
B. So, the king ordered his guards to search for him and bring him back.  
C. Once the king grew displeased with the wise old man, he asked him to leave the village.  
D. The wise old man obeyed the order and left, settling quietly in a nearby town.

**Options:  
**A C D B  
C D A B  
B A C D  
C A D B

**Answer:** C D A B

**Explanation:** Sentence C introduces the situation and serves as the opening. It is logically followed by D, which shows the consequence of the order. A comes next, describing what happened after some time, and B concludes the sequence, indicated by "So," showing the final action.
