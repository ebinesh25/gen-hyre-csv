1.Ravi's \___\___ comments amused his classmates, but annoyed the teacher who expected a serious discussion.

**Options:  
**A. aloof  
B. witty  
C. grave  
D. solemn  
E. stern

__Answer: B. witty__

**Explanation:  
**"Witty" means cleverly humorous or amusing.  
Ravi's comments amused classmates, indicating humor.  
This contrasts with the teacher's expectation of seriousness.
